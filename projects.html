Pennellville, NY Water Supply

The Mobile Home Park, home to 250 residents in 80 homes, urgently needs support to address critical water safety issues. Originally designed in the 1960s, the park's aging water system is struggling to meet current needs. With no water meters in place, daily water usage is estimated at 16,000-20,000 gallons, drawn from four wells.

Recent testing has revealed dangerous levels of PFAS (per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances), with two wells consistently exceeding safety limits, leading to one being shut down. The remaining wells, though still in operation, are not only insufficient to meet demand but also show worrying contamination levels.

As engineers, our role is to design and implement a comprehensive solution that ensures the park's residents have access to safe, clean water. :
