Our Mission

To partner with communities to collaboratively design and implement sustainable engineering projects that improve quality of life. We aim to empower students with hands-on experience, fostering leadership and a commitment to global responsibility, while creating long-term, impactful solutions both locally and internationally.

About Us

We are the officially licensed Brown University chapter of Engineers Without Borders USA, a student-led organization dedicated to developing community-driven solutions for underserved communities locally and internationally. Our chapter uses engineering and social sciences to tackle global challenges, focusing on sustainable projects like water distribution and infrastructure development. Our goal is to help students gain hands-on engineering experience while making a meaningful impact in communities!
